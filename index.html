THE STORY

A destroyed homeland. A promise of revenge.

Primary Force is an original action and fantasy manga created by Yassien Hossam and published on MANGA Plus Creators by SHUEISHA.

The story follows a group of friends whose island is destroyed by a member of the royal family after the protagonist confronts him for exploiting and controlling the island's inhabitants.

After the disaster, the heroes begin a journey of revenge and attempt to change the world. Along the way, they discover that the truth behind their tragedy—and the world itself—is far more complex and mysterious than they expected.

CHARACTERS

The people behind the force

01

Lapy

The main protagonist. Brave, determined, and deeply affected by the destruction of his homeland, Lapy begins his journey seeking revenge while uncovering a truth far more complicated than he expected.

02

Ryu

A calm and powerful fighter known for discipline and sharp judgment. Ryu stays composed in dangerous situations and helps keep the group focused.

03

Ritsu

A mysterious and highly intelligent character with a cold, analytical personality. Ritsu relies on strategy and observation more than raw strength.

04

Nana

A kind-hearted and courageous girl who gives the group hope during difficult moments. Despite her gentle nature, she will fight to protect those she cares about.

05

Mary

A confident, strong-willed fighter with a competitive spirit. Mary refuses to surrender, even in the most dangerous situations.

06

Marianne

One of the story's most mysterious figures. Marianne appears wise and composed, but her past and true intentions remain unclear.

07

Royal Family Member

A powerful royal connected to the destruction of the heroes' homeland. This character embodies authority, corruption, and the abuse of power.
